Last Launcher is a minimal, text-only Android launcher built for speed and simplicity. No icons, no widgets, no clutter — just your apps as plain text labels.

Features:
* Text-only home screen with pinned app shortcuts
* Fast app drawer with instant search
* Task list with search, reorder, and swipe to remove
* Long press for inline actions — rename, pin, hide
* Search-only mode for keyboard-first launching
* System/Light/Dark theme with pure black AMOLED support
* Extra theme with CRT scanline effects and monospace font
* Hide apps you don't want to see
* Auto-launch when a single app matches your search
* Fullscreen mode — hide status bar and navigation
* Multiple languages supported

Last Launcher is free, open source (EUPL-1.2), and contains no ads, tracking, or telemetry. Your data stays on your device.
