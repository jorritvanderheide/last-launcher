# Last Launcher v1.0.0

Text-only Android launcher focused on speed and simplicity.

## What is this?

Last Launcher strips away everything you do not need. No icons, no widgets, no clutter. Just your apps as text labels, and a fast way to find and launch them.

## Features

**Instant app search**
Swipe up to open the app drawer. Type a few letters. When only one app matches, it launches automatically.

**Text-only home screen**
Pin your most-used apps. Rename them. Reorder them. No icons, no previews, just text.

**Integrated task list**
Swipe right from home to access a simple task list. Search, reorder, mark done. No separate app needed.

**Three themes**
System, Light, Dark (pure black AMOLED), and Extra (CRT scanlines and glitch text for the retro crowd).

**Privacy first**
No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no network calls. Your data stays on your device.

**14 languages**
English, Dutch, German, French, Spanish, and more.

## Download

Available on F-Droid and as a sideloadable APK.
